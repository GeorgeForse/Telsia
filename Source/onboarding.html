Welcome to Telsia!

Telsia alleviates digital eye strain and related symptoms by applying a customizable colour overlay.

To begin, pin the Telsia icon to your browser toolbar and select it to open the settings panel.

How to use Telsia

  • Enable/Disable: Turn the overlay on or off using the toggle in the settings panel.
  • Colour: Choose a colour for the overlay - pick a preset or open the colour picker.
  • Intensity: Use the Intensity slider to adjust transparency of the overlay.
  • Presets: Quickly select a pre-configured setting to try recommended colours.

Note: Telsia cannot add overlays to internal browser pages such as the New Tab page or browser settings due to extension security restrictions.

If your extension is managed by an organization, some settings may be disabled by your administrator.
